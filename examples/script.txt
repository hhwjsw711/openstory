FADE IN:

EXT. CITY STREET - NIGHT

Rain pelts the neon-lit pavement. A LONE FIGURE walks through the steam rising from subway grates.

LONE FIGURE
(muttering)
Three days. Three days since she disappeared.

The figure stops under a flickering streetlight, pulls out a crumpled photograph.

CLOSE ON: The photo shows a young woman with bright eyes.

INT. DIVE BAR - CONTINUOUS

The Lone Figure pushes through the door. JAZZ plays from a crackling speaker. Patrons barely look up.

The Figure approaches the BAR, slides onto a stool.

BARTENDER
(polishing glass)
We're closing soon.

LONE FIGURE
I'm looking for someone.

The Bartender's eyes narrow.

FADE OUT.
